Graha and Nakshatra Insights

Understanding Graha (Planets) and Nakshatra (Constellations)

Hindu Jyotish revolves around the interplay of Graha (planets) and Nakshatra (lunar constellations), shaping your destiny:

Navagraha (Nine Planets)

Surya (Sun)

Represents soul, authority, and vitality

Chandra (Moon)

Governs mind, emotions, and intuition

Mangal (Mars)

Fuels energy, courage, and ambition

Budh (Mercury)

Influences intellect and communication

Guru (Jupiter)

Brings wisdom, wealth, and spirituality

Shukra (Venus)

Rules love, luxury, and creativity

Shani (Saturn)

Teaches discipline and karma lessons

Rahu & Ketu

Shadow planets affecting desires and liberation

Pandit Ji's Insight:

Each planet's position in your chart influences specific life areas. Remedies like Poojas and gemstones can harmonize their effects.

Nakshatras (27 Lunar Mansions)

These celestial divisions, like Ashwini, Rohini, and Swati, refine planetary influences. Each Nakshatra has a ruling deity and unique traits, impacting personality and events.

Pandit Ji's Expertise:

With 30 years of experience, he decodes your Nakshatra to offer precise predictions and rituals.
